SYNC · Web Design & Development

SYNC is a project for a fictional festival dedicated to independent and experimental cinema. The visual identity combines dynamism and contemporaneity, designed for a young, creative, and curious audience. During the research and design process, references such as the Edinburgh Film Festival and the Tribeca Film Festival were taken into account.

The goal was to create an attractive and functional static website using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It was developed through the use of modals and hover effects.
