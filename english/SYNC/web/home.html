PROGRAM

SYNC is an international festival of independent and experimental cinema that brings together emerging voices with established filmmakers and artists. The festival offers a contemporary perspective on visual storytelling through film and media art.

ABOUT

THE

VENUE

Located on the banks of the River Spree, Arena Berlin becomes the perfect stage for SYNC — a space where independent cinema, technology and collective experience converge.

Here, boundaries dissolve: the screen merges with architecture, sound travels through the space, and the audience becomes part of the experience.
